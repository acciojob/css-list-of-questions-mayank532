Questions

Que 1 - Who was the first female to become the governor of an Indian state?
Ans 1 - Sarojini Naidu was the first woman to become the governor of an Indian state
. She governed Uttar Pradesh from 15 August 1947 to 2 March 1949.

Que 2 - Who among first Woman to climb Mount Everest twice?
Ans 2 - Santosh Yadav (born 10 October 1967) is an Indian mountaineer.
She is the first woman in the world to climb Mount Everest twice and the first woman to successfully climb Mount Everest from Kangshung Face.

Que 3 - Who is President of India?
Ans 3 - Droupadi Murmu is an Indian politician who has been serving as the 15th president of India since 25 July 2022.
